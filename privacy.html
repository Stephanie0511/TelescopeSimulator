Overview

Telescope Simulator is designed to be a safe and educational experience. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information from users. The app may collect limited, anonymous usage data through standard platform analytics to help improve performance and stability.

How we use information

Any anonymous analytics data is used only to improve app quality, fix bugs, and understand which features are most helpful.

Third-party services

The app may use Google Play Services to distribute updates and provide essential functionality. These services may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Children's privacy

Telescope Simulator is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
