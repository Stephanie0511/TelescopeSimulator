This app presents a realistic 3D simulation of the night sky, modeling over 9,000 stars along with planets and celestial motion. Stars are plotted using real astronomical data, allowing users to observe their positions, brightness, and movement across the celestial sphere. It serves as a cost-effective alternative to physical telescopes, eliminates geographical limitations, and works as a powerful learning tool for understanding astronomy and basic optics. Whether you are discovering the constellations for the first time or deepening your knowledge of celestial motion, this app provides an engaging and informative experience.
